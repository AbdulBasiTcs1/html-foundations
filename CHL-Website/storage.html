What is a Storage Device?

A storage device is hardware that permanently saves your data ‚Äî even when the computer is turned off. This is different from RAM (memory), which loses data when powered off. Storage devices hold your operating system, software applications, documents, photos, videos, and all your files. The two main types are HDD (Hard Disk Drive) and SSD (Solid State Drive).
